That is to say that information will be provided as context to the visualization in the form of a title, comment, or other annotation. A simple element-wise [numerical derivative](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Numerical_differentiation) can be computed producing $dA$: dA = $$\frac{A2_i-A1_i}{t2-t1}$$ $i$ indicates pixel values. $dA$ is a new 3-D array that can be visualized using a 3-D contour plot. ## Plotting Considerations for Multi-Phase Polymer Datasets
